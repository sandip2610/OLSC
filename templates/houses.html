House & Mentoring Program

We focus on holistic development through mentorship and house-based activities.

Our Houses

Students are grouped into different Houses to foster healthy competition, teamwork, and camaraderie. Each House participates in sports, cultural events, and academic challenges throughout the year. This system encourages leadership, responsibility, and team spirit among students.

  • Red House: Excellence in academics
  • Blue House: Strength in sports and fitness
  • Green House: Creativity and innovation
  • Yellow House: Leadership and social service

Mentoring Program

Every student is assigned a mentor who provides guidance on academics, personal development, and career planning. Our mentors help students set goals, overcome challenges, and develop essential life skills. This one-on-one support ensures that each student receives attention and encouragement to excel in all areas of life.

Mentoring sessions include goal-setting, regular feedback, leadership training, and emotional support to ensure holistic growth of students in a nurturing environment.
