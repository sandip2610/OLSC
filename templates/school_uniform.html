School Uniform

Discipline, unity and identity through uniform

At Blogrator Academy, the school uniform represents equality and discipline. Every student is expected to wear the prescribed uniform with pride and dignity.

Boys & Girls Uniform (Class- 1 to Class- 6)

Summer Uniform

White shirt, navy blue trousers, tie, black shoes, school badge.

Winter Uniform

Navy blue blazer, white shirt, tie, grey sweater, black shoes.

Boys & Girls Uniform (Other's)

Summer Uniform

White blouse, navy blue skirt, tie, black shoes, school badge.

Winter Uniform

Navy blue blazer, white blouse, grey sweater, skirt/trousers, black shoes.
